Bogotá Segura

Bogotá Segura was a project developed for my middle career project in fifth semester. The project consisted of the development of a hybrid modile and web app, which helped the citizens from Bogotá to report crimes from their phones or from their computer by using the web app. It's development reached an approximate of 60% of its full functionality.

This view represents the app's home page, it offers the following functionalities: Report an incident, view the reported incidents, open the dynamic incidents map, see statistics and send an alert to nearby police officers

This page represents the app's login page, it offered the possibility of logging in using face recognition (Iphone X or supperior) and biometrics (Fingerprint recognition).

This view represents the dynamic incident map, which shows, depending on the quantity of incidents on a given zone, different colors (green, yellow and red), representing how dangerous each zone is.
